    Skills and/or Knowledge utilized¶

    Psionics¶

    What are Psionics?1

    Psionic is a word invented in the 20th century as an umbrella term to describe human paranormal behavior. It refers to all powers of the mind—from the passive (telepathy or clairvoyance) to the active (telekinesis or pyrokinesis). Psionics is the study of all these powers2.

    Psychic Element Manipulation¶

    • The power to manipulate elements of pure psychic energy. Combination of Psychic Energy Manipulation and Elemental Manipulation.
    • Invoked by 'focusing' on the element type and the effect that needs to be accomplished.
    • Not instantaneous.
    • Teletechnics3 can be used for instantaneous effects

    Element Types

    • Air
      • Smoke
    • Earth
      • Crystal
      • Metal
    • Fire
    • Water
      • Ice
    • Light
      • Darkness (Shadow)

    Psychic Energy Manipulation¶

    • The ability to manipulate psychic energy. Variation of Energy Manipulation.

    Energy Types

    • Healing
    • Shield
    • Energy Drain
      • Psychic
      • Life
