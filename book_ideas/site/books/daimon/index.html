    Daimon

    Chapter 1

    Highlights
    1. Intro to hero, Eamon O'Connell.
    2. Witnessing the battle between Marshal and Violator.
    3. Helping turn the tide, but horribly wounded. Also, Marshal does not survive.
    4. Eamon O'Connell inherits responsibilities, tech equipment, and AI assistant, named Apkallu.
    5. AI in form of bracer on left forearm. Can change shape & form.
    6. Two Violators enter the hospital to seize the AI and kill Eamon O'Connell
    7. Escape via Psychic Energy Manipulation activation by draining of one of the Violators as fuel. Energy Drain (Life).
      • However, The use of Energy Drain (Life) causes additional conflicts.
      • Some will believe It should not have been unlocked without binding oaths and punishments.
      • The reason for the fear is that using Energy Drain (Life) and Energy Drain (Psychic) can add the target's skillset to the originators.
    8. Part of conflict caught on hospital internal security video feed, and police/feds involved. A Paladin is with them.

    Chapter 2

    Highlights
    1. Intro to villain, Donal Fitzgerald.
    2. Hunts other Daimons to equip those loyal to his faction.
    3. Mindset is psychopathic corporate raider. Targets other factions for tech and resources.
    4. Pulls hospital video surveillance videos.
    5. Starts to hunt Eamon O'Connell. Calls forth "The Wild Hunt."
    6. Seeks to learn how to create a Dark Paladin daimon that is loyal/subservient to him.

    Chapter 3

    Highlights
    1. Eamon O'Connell goes back home to visit family & friends.
    2. Discovers a previous girlfriend is also part-daimon (part of clan Draoi). Small clan of elemental-type Daimon of Celtic background.
    3. She helps him and helps him realize his potential, and that having a personal AI is overpowered for him. Potentially a or type daimon.
    4. By sharing his energy, Eamon O'Connell starts to unlock additional elemental affinities within Gwenllian (Gwen) Draoi.
